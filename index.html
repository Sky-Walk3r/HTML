The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

I had been staring out of my window for about an hour now. On my desk, lying between copies of Nature, New Scientists, and all the other scientific journals my work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been my favourite book since college, and the longer i spent in these four walls the more i felt i needed to be free.

I had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered my pioneering work, unraveling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, I did think i had something batter to do.
